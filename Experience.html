Experience

Over the past five years, I have cultivated a thriving freelance career in photography, specializing in capturing the essence of diverse subjects through my lens. My journey began with a focus on event photography, where I honed my skills in documenting special moments with an acute attention to detail. As my portfolio expanded, I delved into portrait photography, collaborating with individuals to convey their unique personalities and stories through captivating images. This evolution allowed me to explore various styles and techniques, from candid shots that encapsulate genuine emotions to carefully crafted compositions that tell a visual narrative. Embracing the digital era, I proficiently navigate post-production processes, ensuring that my work not only meets but exceeds the expectations of my clients. With a commitment to continuous improvement and a passion for visual storytelling, I thrive in the dynamic world of freelancing, where each project presents an opportunity to create lasting memories through the artistry of photography.
